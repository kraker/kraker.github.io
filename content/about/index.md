Red Hat Enterprise Linux practitioner based in Tulsa, OK. I spend my
time on the boring parts of Linux infrastructure — the parts that
keep systems up, audits clean, and on-call quiet.

## What I do

- **Consulting.** Building a practice focused on RHEL-based
  environments: system administration, security hardening, automation,
  and the unglamorous day-two work.
- **Writing.** *The RHCSA Field Manual*, an opinionated study guide
  for people preparing for RHCSA certification —
  [GitHub](https://github.com/kraker/rhcsa-field-manual).
- **Open source.** Maintaining
  [kraker/rhcsa](https://github.com/kraker/rhcsa), the upstream
  community resource the Field Manual draws from. Past contributions
  to the [Linux Upskill Challenge](https://linuxupskillchallenge.org/)
  and [PaperStreet](https://github.com/paperstreetco/).

## Off the clock

Piano, fiddle, hiking, camping. One dog named Feather.
